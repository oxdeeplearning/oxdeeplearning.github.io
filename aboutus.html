About Us

The Club’s main purpose is to raise Oxford students’ awareness as well as addressing the needs of those who are interested but lack the resources and opportunities to approach this field. This is because despite the rapid development of AI, the general public’s understanding of it does not seem to be up to speed. We have noticed that many students, including those not from the faculty of Computer Science, have expressed interests in artificial intelligence and are eager to learn about it. Particularly, we reckon that students who are doing Sciences, Engineering or Psychology may indeed already possess decent transferable knowledge to support their study on AI, but only need more resources and guidance. We therefore wish to pioneer a machine learning club in Oxford, to bring together all students who are interested, and assist them in catching up with the rapid technological progress. We believe that AI is not merely about computer science, but rather it is an interdisciplinary subject which requires the contribution by students from different fields of study. Thus, our club welcomes students from all disciplines, to bring in new insights and hence achieving more together.
